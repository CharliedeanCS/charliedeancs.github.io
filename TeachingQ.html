Camp Thailand Teaching Qualifications:

During your time at Camp Thailand, you will spend time in classrooms working with local Thai children; check out our teaching qualifications on offer for Camp Thailand customers:

30 Hour Teaching Qualification
FREE For Camp Thailand Customers

The teaching qualifications that Camp provides are from an accredited distinguished member of ACDL (Accreditation Council for Distance Learning) as well as an approved training centre with the TQUK (Training Qualifications UK), an Ofqual-endorsed Awarding Organisation (Office of Qualifications and Examinations Regulation, England).

Being fully accredited means that our education courses are of an extremely high standard and internationally recognised; this way you can be confident in your overseas job search, knowing you’ve achieved a lifelong, quality qualification. Our independently accredited courses will make sure that your training meets international standards in quality and deliverance.

Here at Camp we include a 30-hour Teaching Young Learners Course in your application which is an essential part of your application. You will gain access to all the course material and have assistance all the way. Please note this course MUST be completed before arriving at your Camp Location.

Study time will begin once registration is complete and you’ll have 90 days to complete all sections of your course. Got an academic or technical question? Contact Premier TEFL and the team will be happy to help!

120 Hour Teaching Qualification
Only £99 For Camp Thailand Customers

Camp customers qualify for a 120-hour advanced TEFL course at a reduced rate of £99.

The course is accredited by the ACDL (Accreditation Council for Distance Learners), as well as TQUK (Training Qualifications UK); the document is an internationally accredited certification and can be accessed on any device.

Goabroad.com, the international program provider affiliated with this course, are a Star Verified Organisation, with the award recognising their dedication to world wide education.

Spread across a 3-month study period, the 120-hour advanced TEFL course offers a flexible study schedule, as well as support from a personal tutor; students can also provided with supplementary resources with a 70+ page Grammar eBook included.


IMPORTANT: If you subscribe to either of the above qualifications and do not pay for your Camp program in full then you will be liable for the full retail price of the qualification.
